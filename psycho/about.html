Working with Me

Let’s be real. Living a life marked by honesty and realness takes the guesswork out of approaching novel situations and relationships.

I know from firsthand experience how fragmented we can feel without reflection and self-discovery. I work with individuals to identify these cycles and decide where to disrupt them.

My Approach

All emotions are valid

We tend to question our emotions in efforts to clear up confusion, but I find this actually leads to even more uncertainty. I encourage patients to think of their emotions as valid but stemming from possible irrational thoughts. In changing our thoughts, we can develop more positive feelings

Emotions are instinct

In situations or relationships we’ve never been in before, all we have are our instincts. I think of emotions as our instincts and by nurturing our relationship with them, we will always be equipped to take on whatever is in front of us. Knowing our instincts means knowing ourselves.

Tough love doesn’t work

In order to push ourselves and achieve our goals, we tend to be hard on ourselves. But we go too far and judge ourselves for our efforts in the process. I help others in understanding how this is actually an obstacle rather than a motivator and encourage self-compassion and acknowledging of strengths.

It’s all connected

So much of our distress comes from feeling fragmented and not knowing ourselves. I help patients look at the pieces of themselves and discover how they fit together. Even if we may not know the end result, this process can be incredibly exciting and rewarding.
